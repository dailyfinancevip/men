Convenient Appointments

We’re available 5 days a week and have specific office hours listed to the right. Outside of normal office hours you can call us to receive an on call nurse, if needed.

Due to COVID19, we are working hard to protect our staff and patients. If you have an upcoming appointment in the next month, and can answer yes to any of the following questions, we kindly ask that you call to reschedule to a later date.

  • Have you traveled outside the Amarillo area in the last 14 days?
  • Have you or anyone else who lives in the home, traveled outside the United States in the last 21 days?
  • Do you currently have a fever or experienced ANY respiratory symptoms such as a cough, wheezing or shortness of breath?

Thank you and stay well and stay safe!
